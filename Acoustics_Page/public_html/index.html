College of Engineering, Technology, and Architecture

Discover What's Next for Your Future

Recognized by Business Insider as one of the nation’s top 15 engineering schools when it comes to mid-career salary earnings, the College of Engineering, Technology, and Architecture (CETA) attracts students from around the country and across the world. Here, you’ll find undergraduate and graduate programs that will bring you to the forefront of your field through exceptional teaching and many opportunities for industry and faculty-led research and internships. 

The way we combine our small classes with career-ready tactics like industry job fairs, mock interviews, and networking programs with our many industry partners, means you’ll emerge ready to make the transition to a successful professional career or graduate school. 

Innovation Lives Here

You can benefit from our long history of students and faculty who are committed to innovation— and the patents hanging in our school hallways prove it. We’ve worked on everything from developing the next generation of cell phone antennas, to improving helmets and spacesuits for NASA, to designing buildings with minimal environmental impact. You’ll have an opportunity to work on some of the world’s most complex and thought-provoking problems before you even graduate. 

Take a look at our areas of study and see how far CETA can help you discover what’s next.
